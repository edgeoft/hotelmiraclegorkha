Welcome to Hotel Miracle

Welcome to Hotel Miracle, your business class destination in the heart of Gorkha, Nepal. We offer modern comfort, exceptional hospitality, and a prime location close to the city’s major attractions and business centers. Whether you’re visiting for business or leisure, our dedicated team ensures a memorable and relaxing stay with personalized service and top-notch amenities.

  • Spacious, modern rooms & suites
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi
  • Friendly, professional staff
  • Conference & meeting hall facilities

Community Contribution

Proud Moments at Hotel Miracle

At Hotel Miracle, we believe in giving back to our community. Together with our dedicated staff, we proudly donated the money collected in our Tips Box to Manav Sewa Ashram Gorkha—a non-profit organization devoted to helping and rescuing disabled, homeless, and helpless people across Nepal. We are honored to support their noble mission and make a positive impact in the lives of those in need.
